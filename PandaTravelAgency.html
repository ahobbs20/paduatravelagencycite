Longmen Grottoes, China

Overview

The Longmen Grottoes are located on both sides of the Yi River. They are also just south of the ancient capital of Luoyang, Henan province. The Longmen Grottoes have more than 2,300 caves and alcoves that are carved into steep limestone cliffs. They contain almost 110,000 Buddhist stone statues. Some of the most intense carving was done from the 5th century to the mid-8th century. You can look here for more information about the history of Longmen Grottoes!
